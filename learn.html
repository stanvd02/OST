Democratizing Technology

At OST, we believe that technology should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their coding knowledge or background. Our mission is to break down barriers and empower individuals to create, innovate, and solve problems using technology.

For Everyone

We create tools that are intuitive and accessible, making technology available to students, educators, creators, and professionals alike.

No Coding Required

Our visual tools and intuitive interfaces allow anyone to build and create, without needing to learn complex programming languages.

Open Source

All our tools are free and open source, fostering a community of collaboration and continuous improvement.

Our Values

Accessibility

Making technology accessible to everyone, everywhere, regardless of their background or resources.

Education

Empowering learning through intuitive tools that make technology concepts easy to understand.

Community

Building a supportive community where everyone can learn, share, and grow together.

Innovation

Continuously improving our tools and creating new solutions to meet evolving needs.
